# Qdrant Reward Radar Video Design

## Style Prompt

A crisp technical operator dashboard video on a light canvas. Use structured grids, restrained motion, clear data hierarchy, and sharp table-like route cards. The tone should feel credible for a developer hackathon submission, not like marketing fluff.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#f5f7fb`
- Panel: `#ffffff`
- Ink: `#17202a`
- Muted text: `#5c6675`
- Qdrant teal: `#0f766e`
- Signal blue: `#2563eb`
- Success green: `#15803d`
- Review amber: `#b45309`
- Block red: `#b91c1c`
- Grid line: `#d7dde6`

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `Helvetica Neue`, `Arial`, sans-serif.
- Use compact dashboard-scale headings, never oversized hero type after the title scene.

## Motion

- Panels enter with short vertical or horizontal movement and opacity.
- Transition between scenes using clean full-width wipes.
- No looping infinite animation.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use dark blue/slate as the dominant theme.
- Do not use gradient blobs, bokeh, or decorative orbs.
- Do not use rounded cards above 8px radius.
- Do not rely on stock imagery or abstract mascot illustrations.
